Frequently Asked Questions

How many players can I add?
The app supports 2 to 10 players per game.

Can I fix a score I entered wrong?
Yes! On the scoreboard, tap the pencil icon under any round label to edit that round's scores.

Does the app save my game if I close it?
The current version does not persist games between app launches. A future update will add this feature.

Is this app affiliated with the 5 Crowns card game?
No. This is an unofficial score-tracking utility. 5 Crowns is a trademark and copyrighted product of Cannei, LLC.
