5 Crowns Scorekeeper

Privacy Policy

Short version: This app stores your game data on your device and, if you are signed into iCloud, optionally syncs it across your devices using Apple's private CloudKit database. We do not collect, sell, or share your data.

Overview

5 Crowns Scorekeeper ("the App") is a score-tracking utility for the card game 5 Crowns. It is developed and maintained by Randy Cox ("we", "us", "our").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App handles and how.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any data with third-party advertisers or data brokers

Data Storage & iCloud Sync

Player names, game scores, and game history are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you are signed into iCloud and have iCloud enabled for this app.

If you are signed into iCloud, the App uses Apple CloudKit to sync your game history and statistics across your personal devices. This means:

  • Your data is stored in your private iCloud account, not on our servers
  • Randy Cox has no access to your iCloud data at any time
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → 5 Crowns Scorekeeper
Apple's handling of iCloud and CloudKit data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

The App offers an optional subscription via the Apple App Store. All payment transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment or billing information.

Subscription management is governed by Apple's Standard End User License Agreement.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Apple-provided services only:

  • Apple CloudKit — private iCloud data sync (see above)
  • Apple StoreKit — subscription and purchase management

No third-party analytics platforms, advertising networks, crash-reporting SDKs, or social media services are integrated into the App.

Data Deletion

To delete your local game history, use the Clear All option within the Stats & History screen in the App.

To delete your iCloud data, go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → 5 Crowns Scorekeeper → Delete Data.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
