5 Crowns Scorekeeper

Privacy Policy

Short version: This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data stays on your device.

Overview

5 Crowns Scorekeeper ("the App") is a simple score-tracking utility for the card game 5 Crowns. It is developed and maintained by Randy Cox ("we", "us", "our").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App handles and how.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Player names or scores entered into the App

Data Storage

Any player names and scores you enter are stored only on your device for the duration of a game session. No data is sent to any server or third party at any time.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media services. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information, there is no special risk to children's privacy. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Effective Date" below and post the revised policy at this URL. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
